Our Communication Network

Our subsidiary companies provide branding, new product development, strategic planning, media planning, creative advertising design, TV commercial, in-store communication, direct and promotional marketing, and digital & interactive communication services to clients throughout Asia Pacific region in various industries.

As a holding company, NeuMerlin Group manages a portfolio of service companies in particular fields of specialities.

Our Value

Our core value is “CREATIFICIENCY” that roots deep in our working philosophy and vision of our brands. This value is attributed from enhanced working ecology with multidisciplinary team formation. We always consider things in a holistic view, balancing between rationale and creativity to maximize CREATIFICIENCY that translates to unique qualities in our works of each speciality.

We are mindful at microscopic level yet focusing on delivering an integrated solution as a whole. The goal is to transform any ideation to implementation that meets all given objectives. By simply saying solution, we mean a solution that has been derived from a well-thought-through thinking process and can be executed holistically, aesthetically, and meaningfully.

Providing good services seems to be too stereotypical! Here, we strive for greatness. We try hard not to over-promise, yet try harder to over-deliver. We would rather concentrate on the end result at which the true performance is measured. Understanding what is Good, is a foundation of what is Great. From that, tremendous creative efforts are put in to bring good work to greatness.
